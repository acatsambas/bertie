Bertie Data Request
If you wish to delete your account data, please follow these steps:
  1. Log into the app
  2. Tap on your profile icon at the top right
  3. Tap 'Delete Account'
  4. Type begone! in the text box
  5. Tap Delete account
Please note that this will delete all the data we hold for you, including your reading list. We won't be able to retrieve this data for you if you wish to reinstate your account.

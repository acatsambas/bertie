Bertie Support

To use Bertie, start by downloading the app and signing up. You can then use the bottom sheet to:

  • View and update your reading list
  • Discover bookshops and new books to read
  • Place an order

To add books to your list, go to My list, and tap at the bar at the top of the page (the one that says + Search for a book). You can add books to your list by tapping the plus button next to the results, or by opening a book and then tapping Add to list.

To order a book, tap Order in the bottom sheet. Note that you will need to add your address and have saved at least one bookstore in your favourites to place an order.
